SHANGRI-LA - CHINA

Today is a popular place for hiking and horse riding in the surrounding area. In the city on the top of the mountain there is a monastery, next to it is the largest prayer drum in the world with a height of 21 m. To the north of the city is a large monastery with a variety of attractions.

BALI - INDONESIA

Organize your trip to Indonesia. Also known as the island of the gods, Bali is a tourist destination located on the islands of Indonesia, ideal for all types of travelers: backpackers, explorers, adventurers or those who are just looking for a relaxing trip in a paradisiacal environment. Bali may seem like a small place to travel to (the entire island of Bali can be covered by car in one day) but its importance as a tourist destination is enormous.

UBUD - INDONESIA

Ubud is an almost mandatory stop for any traveler in Bali. In Ubud there is no beach, however, it is much calmer and more relaxing than the south of the island. You will also notice the different atmosphere (it is cooler) and the people: many villagers greet you on the street and give you a smile as they pass by. There are plenty of temples, museums, and art galleries. For this reason, it is considered the cultural center of Bali.
